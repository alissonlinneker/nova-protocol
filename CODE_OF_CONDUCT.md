# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is maintained by professionals who value direct, constructive communication. We hold ourselves and each other to a high standard of technical discourse.

### Expected Behavior

- Focus on the technical merits of ideas, not the person presenting them.
- Provide constructive feedback with concrete suggestions.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Prioritize what is best for the project and its users.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, inflammatory remarks, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, chat channels, and any other forums where you represent this project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@novaprotocol.io**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate.
